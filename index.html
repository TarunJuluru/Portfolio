about me

I started my career in design industry as I’m one of them who believe that a simple, minimal and usable experiences that carried with right emotional state will help people feel destructive-free and build focus in experiecce the world better way, And as designing such experiences is really challenging for me, I like the iterative design process in drawing solution to the problem following the best practices possible.

My interest towards UX/UI design has envolved with my final year of graduation where I have been introduced to the Front-end development(Html/CSS) as one of our computer science languages and that explorations lead me to the path of UX/UI design in which, I decided I could provide a real value for. Check into my Resume or linkedin profile to know more about my education.

Design is something I'm always interested in. And I do have a good eye for it where that enthusiasm lead me to explore even more into it. My long term goal is to grow as advanced motion graphic designer in which I believe that I can bridge the gap between user's emotional state in communicatinting their ideas through visual medium.
