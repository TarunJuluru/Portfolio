why?

As most of the educational institutes being followed by the traditional theory-based teaching methods, Bitlab Academy is one of the first innovative educational institution, that offers several different industry-leading and in-demand educational programs, followed by alternative project-based, peer-to-peer learning methodology with smooth LMS [learning managing system] support. Bitlab also offers flexible tuition fee and career network understanding that students comes from different financial background need to received world-class education and opportunities considering each individual determination and practice at the first place.
